Public Links

Every project gets a unique, public URL that you can share with friends, colleagues, or customers.

https://gorillabuilder.dev/app/your-project-name-1234

You can find this link by clicking the "Open in New Tab" icon in the top-right corner of the Editor preview pane.

"Sleeping" Apps & Cold Starts

To keep our service affordable, apps "go to sleep" after a period of inactivity.

  • The Wake-Up Process: When someone visits your link, they might see a "Waking up app..." screen for a few seconds.
  • This is normal: Our backend is spinning up the container just for them.
  • Auto-Fix on Boot: If the app crashed while sleeping, the wake-up process will even try to auto-repair it before the user sees it!

Custom Subdomains

Currently, subdomains are generated based on your project name. Custom domains (e.g., `yourname.com`) are coming soon for Enterprise users.
