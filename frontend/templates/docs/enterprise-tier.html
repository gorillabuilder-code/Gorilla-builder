The Enterprise Tier

For agencies, dev shops, and massive scale operations that require total control, isolation, and guaranteed throughput.

Scaling Beyond the Indie Dev

While the Free and Premium tiers are perfectly tuned for indie hackers and solo founders, Gor://a Builder's underlying orchestration engine is built to handle enterprise-grade complexity. If your agency is generating hundreds of applications, or your organization requires strict data compliance, the Enterprise Tier removes all guardrails.

Enterprise Features

The Enterprise Tier replaces standard consumer limits with custom infrastructure allocations:

  • Custom VPC Hosting: Run the WebContainers and the Gor://a backend orchestration engine within your own Virtual Private Cloud. Your code and prompts never touch our public architecture.
  • Dedicated Model Instances: Avoid global rate limits. We spin up dedicated throughput instances for the LLMs powering the Swarm (Planner, Coder, Debugger) to guarantee lightning-fast response times during peak hours.
  • SSO & SAML: Advanced security and team member provisioning. Connect your Okta or Azure AD to manage developer access.
  • Custom Component Injection: Train the Swarm on your company's proprietary component library. Instead of defaulting to Shadcn/UI, the Coder agent will natively import and build with your internal design system.
  • Custom SLAs: Guaranteed uptime, dedicated technical account managers, and priority auto-healing support.

On-Premise Deployment: For organizations with strict compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOC2, etc.), the entire Gor://a Builder multi-agent framework can be containerized and shipped for On-Premise deployment.
