Daily Spin the Wheel

A little rebellion against the SaaS status quo. Wager your tokens to multiply your runway.

Gamifying the Free Tier

We built Gor://a Builder for the indie hackers, the tinkerers, and the builders working late into the night. We know what it's like to be 5,000 tokens short of finishing a massive feature, with no budget to upgrade to a Pro tier.

Instead of locking you out entirely, we introduced a completely unique economic mechanic: The Daily Spin.

How It Works

Once every 24 hours, a modal will unlock on your Dashboard. You can choose to wager a portion of your existing Live Credits (up to 500,000 tokens).

When you spin the wheel, a server-side cryptographic roll determines your fate:

  • 50% Chance - The Bust (0x): You lose the tokens you wagered. The house takes them.
  • 30% Chance - The Boost (1.5x): You win back your wager plus a 50% profit.
  • 20% Chance - The Jackpot (2.0x): You double your wagered tokens instantly.

Sustainable Prototyping: If you are lucky, you can theoretically sustain your Free Tier usage indefinitely by continually winning the Daily Spin. It's our way of giving persistent developers a fighting chance to finish their MVP without reaching for their wallet.

The Mechanics

To ensure fairness, the math is processed entirely on the backend in app.py. The frontend UI utilizes a beautiful conic-gradient CSS animation to spin the wheel visually, but the actual token deduction or addition is handled by a secure database transaction the moment you click "Spin".

You cannot spin more than once a day. The system checks your last_spin_date in the PostgreSQL database against UTC time. Use it wisely.
