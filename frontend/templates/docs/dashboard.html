Navigating the Dashboard

The Dashboard is the indie developer's central command center. It is designed to give you instant access to your active workspaces, current token limits, and AI configuration settings, ensuring you never hit unexpected roadblocks.

The Anatomy of the Dashboard

The interface is split into three core zones:

  1. The Sidebar: Contains your global navigation (Projects, Agent Skills, Settings, Billing, and Docs). Your user profile and current plan status are anchored securely at the bottom.
  2. The Command Bar: The large input field where you define new app ideas, upload image references, or toggle the Supabase provisioning switch.
  3. The Projects Grid: A visual library of all your generated applications.

Managing Projects & Visual Snapshots

Every time you generate an app, it appears as a card in your Projects Grid. To help you manage multiple ideas, Gor://a takes a dynamic Visual Snapshot of your application's UI during the build process. You can instantly identify your projects visually rather than relying on generic titles.

  • Opening a Project: Click anywhere on the card to launch the WebContainer IDE, rehydrate your Node.js environment, and resume your session.
  • Deleting a Project: Hover over the snapshot image to reveal the red icon.

Warning on Deletion: Deleting a project permanently wipes the virtual file system and chat history from the Gor://a databases. However, if a remote Supabase Postgres database was provisioned for the project, it is left active in your personal Supabase account to ensure your data is never accidentally destroyed.

The Token Economy: 500k Free Tokens

At the top of the dashboard, you will see your Live Credits. We know how frustrating it is to be hit with a paywall after three messages. That's why every Gor://a user gets a massive 500,000 free tokens to start.

Every time the AI generates code, parses a Figma file, reads an image, or transcodes audio, tokens are deducted from this balance. Keeping an eye on this ensures your workflow isn't interrupted.

Gamification: Spin & Haggle

We believe development should be fun, and economics should be in the developer's favor:

  • Daily Spin the Wheel: Every 24 hours, a modal will appear allowing you to wager your existing tokens. You have a chance to multiply your wager by 1.5x or 2.0x, allowing you to sustain your free tier usage indefinitely if you're lucky!
  • The Monke Negotiator: Looking for a discount on the Premium tier? Click the "Haggle with Monke" badge in the sidebar to negotiate your subscription price with an aggressive AI business gorilla.
