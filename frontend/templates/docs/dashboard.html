The Dashboard

Your Dashboard is command central. Here you can manage your projects, check your credit usage, and upgrade your plan.

1. The Builder Bar

At the center of the screen is the Builder Bar. This is where every project starts. Enter a prompt here to spawn a new environment.

Tip: You can click the "Suggestion Chips" below the bar to auto-fill common ideas.

2. Project Cards

Your recent projects appear as cards in the grid below. Each card shows:

  • Project Name
  • Brief Description (generated from your prompt)
  • Last Edited Date

Click Open to enter the standard editor.

3. Credit Widget

In the top right, you will see your Token Balance. Every time the AI writes code or fixes a bug, it consumes tokens.

  • Free Plan: Limited tokens per month.
  • Premium Plan: Higher limits and access to X-Mode.
Run out of tokens? You can top up at any time or wait for the monthly reset.
