Free vs. Premium & Enterprise

Most AI coding platforms act as thin wrappers, luring you in before demanding $30 or $40 a month just to spin up a backend. Gor://a Builder's unit economics are fundamentally different. Our Unified AI & Auth Gateway slashes integration overhead, allowing us to pass those savings directly to you.

The Free Tier (For the Indie Dev)

Every new user starts on the Free Tier. We utilize an ad-subsidized model so that the platform remains profitable for us, and completely free for you to build your SaaS.

  • 3 Project Limit: Maintain up to 3 active full-stack workspaces simultaneously. (Delete an old project to make room for a new one).
  • 500,000 Free Tokens: A generous starting quota, enough to generate dozens of complex components, API routes, and database schemas.
  • Standard Models: Access to incredibly fast, highly-capable models optimized for rapid UI iteration and Express.js backend generation.
  • Unified Gateway Access: Free access to inject OAuth, LLMs, and image generation into your apps without providing your own API keys.

The Premium (Pro) Tier: $13.99/mo

For serious developers, founders, and designers who want to strip away the ads and maximize their throughput. At a fraction of the cost of competitors, you get the entire world.

  • Unlimited Projects: Build, experiment, and store as many applications as your heart desires.
  • 5,000,000 Monthly Tokens: Massive context windows and virtually unlimited coding capacity.
  • Xmode Access: Unlocks the absolute best coding LLMs known to mankind (such as Claude 3.5 Sonnet or DeepSeek V3) for handling massive refactors, complex state management, and deep architectural reasoning.
  • Ad-Free Experience: Removes all banner ads from the dashboard and editor for a clean, distraction-free environment.
  • ZIP Exports & Deployments: Unlocks the ability to download your raw React/Node.js source code locally, or deploy your app via GitHub to Vercel with custom domains.

Haggle with Monke: Want the Pro plan but short on cash? Look for the "Haggle with Monke" button in the dashboard. If you can out-negotiate our chain-smoking AI business gorilla, you can get your first month for as low as $0.67. Good luck.

The Enterprise Tier

For agencies, dev shops, and larger teams that require scale, security, and complete isolation.

  • Custom VPCs: WebContainers and backends hosted within your own Virtual Private Cloud.
  • SSO & SAML: Advanced security and team member provisioning.
  • Dedicated Model Hosting: Avoid rate limits with dedicated throughput for your Swarm agents.
  • Custom SLAs: Guaranteed uptime, dedicated engineering support, and on-premise deployment options.

To upgrade to Enterprise, please contact our sales team directly through the Billing portal.
