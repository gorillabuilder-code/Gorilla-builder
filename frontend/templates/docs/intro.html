Welcome to Gorilla Builder

Gorilla Builder is an AI-powered development environment designed to take you from a simple text prompt to a fully deployed web application in seconds. We combine the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) with a live, sandboxed development environment.

Note: You do not need to know how to code to use Gorilla, but knowing basic HTML/CSS/JS can help you refine your apps.

How it Works

The process is simple:

  1. Prompt: You describe your idea (e.g., "A personal finance tracker with charts").
  2. Plan: Our AI Planner breaks your idea down into actionable steps.
  3. Code: The AI Coder writes the files (HTML, CSS, JS, React, etc.).
  4. Preview: The server boots up instantly, letting you interact with your app.

Quick Start

To create your first project:

  1. Go to your Dashboard.
  2. Type your idea into the main input box.
  3. Click the Arrow button.
  4. Watch as the AI builds your project in real-time.
