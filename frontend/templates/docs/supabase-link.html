Linking Your Supabase Account

Granting the Swarm permission to act as your dedicated DevOps engineer and Database Administrator.

Why Supabase?

To build a production-ready SaaS, you need a production-ready database. We chose Supabase because it provides scalable PostgreSQL databases, an incredible REST API, and native edge functions. However, manually setting up a database, finding your API keys, writing the schema, and injecting it into your app takes hours.

Gor://a Builder turns this into a zero-click operation via OAuth Integration.

The OAuth Handshake

To allow Gor://a to provision databases for you, you must link your account using the Supabase Management API.

  1. Navigate to Account & Settings in the Gor://a Dashboard.
  2. Click the Link Supabase Account button.
  3. You will be securely redirected to Supabase to authorize Gor://a Builder.
  4. Once authorized, Supabase hands Gor://a a secure Management Token, allowing our backend to interact with your organization.

Security & Scopes: We take your security seriously. Gor://a Builder only uses this Management Token to create *new* projects and execute SQL migrations on the specific databases it generates for you. It does not tamper with your pre-existing manual Supabase projects.

Activating the Database Mode

Once linked, a new superpower is unlocked on your Dashboard. In the main prompt bar, you will notice a Supabase Toggle Button (the green circular icon).

When you are about to prompt an app that requires a database (e.g., "Build a CRM that tracks customer purchases"), simply click the Supabase toggle to turn it ON. This sends a hidden is_db_request=true flag to the orchestration engine, kicking off the Autonomous Provisioning sequence.

Alternatively, if you type keywords like "database", "postgres", or "supabase" in your prompt, the AI will naturally infer the requirement, provided your account is linked.
